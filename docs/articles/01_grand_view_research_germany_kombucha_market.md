# Germany Kombucha Market Size | Industry Report, 2033
**Source:** Grand View Research
**URL:** https://www.grandviewresearch.com/industry-analysis/germany-kombucha-market-report
**Date accessed:** March 2026

## Key Findings

- Germany kombucha market was valued at **USD 199.4 million in 2024**
- Projected to reach **USD 606.4 million by 2033**
- CAGR of **13.2%** (2025–2033)
- Germany is the **largest national kombucha market in Europe**, accounting for 25.7% of the European total in 2024

## Market Segments

- **Conventional kombucha** dominates with 91.4% market share (2024)
- **Hard kombucha** is fastest-growing segment, projected CAGR of 17.3% (2025–2033)
- **On-trade** (bars, restaurants, cafés) holds largest distribution share
- **Off-trade** (retail) is fastest-growing channel (CAGR 13.8%)

## Growth Drivers

- Rising awareness of gut health and probiotics
- Shift away from sugary soft drinks
- Growth of "mindful drinking" / low-alcohol lifestyle
- Increasing availability in mainstream supermarkets and discount chains
