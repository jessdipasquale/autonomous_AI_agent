## Key Findings

- Booch News Worldwide Directory reports **31 brands** currently competing in the German kombucha market
- Germany is one of the most covered markets in the global kombucha trade press
- Strong concentration of brands in **Berlin** and **Munich**

## Brand Profiles Covered

### ROY Kombucha (Berlin)
- Founded by Fabio Carlucci, launched summer 2019
- Certified organic, handcrafted in Berlin
- Packaged in cans — modern, design-forward positioning
- ~7,877 Instagram followers
- Available at roykombucha.com and select Berlin retailers

### Bärbucha / Café Bärbucha (Berlin, Schöneberg)
- Europe's first dedicated kombucha café and fermentery
- Offers 20+ raw kombucha and Jun varieties
- Also sells fermented foods: kvass, kefir, lacto-pickles
- Strong in-store/experiential brand; less mass-market

### Fairment (Berlin)
- Founded 2015/2016
- Focuses on SCOBY cultures and home brewing kits as well as finished kombucha
- B2B and B2C channels; strong e-commerce
- Member of Kombucha Brewers International

### Kombuco Fizz (Munich)
- Munich-based artisanal producer
- Lower public profile than Berlin brands
