# Europe Kombucha Market Report (Germany focus)
**Source:** Market Data Forecast
**URL:** https://www.marketdataforecast.com/market-reports/europe-kombucha-market
**Date accessed:** March 2026

## Key Findings

- European kombucha market estimated at **USD 1.04 billion in 2024**
- Projected to reach **USD 1.24 billion by 2025**, expanding to **USD 5.41 billion by 2033**
- CAGR of **20.07%** (2025–2033) — fastest estimate for the European region
- **Germany, UK, and France** are the three leading markets

## Germany's Position

- Germany leads Europe by kombucha market share (25.7% in 2024)
- Germany leads in **per capita consumption**, partly due to cultural familiarity with fermented foods
- Germany's mainstream grocery retail (REWE, EDEKA, Aldi, Lidl) increasingly stocking kombucha

## Pricing Dynamics

Price benchmarks in mainstream European retail:
- Remedy: €2.49–€2.79 per bottle
- GUTsy Captain: €2.50–€2.99 per bottle
- Private label: €1.99–€2.49 per bottle

## Competitive Pressure

- 31 brands identified competing in Germany (Booch News Worldwide Directory)
- Private label products entering at lower price points are increasing competition
- International brands (Remedy from Australia, GUTsy Captain from Portugal) taking German shelf space
